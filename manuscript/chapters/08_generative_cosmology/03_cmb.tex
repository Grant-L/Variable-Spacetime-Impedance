\section{The CMB as the Thermodynamic Attractor (Latent Heat)}

If the universe is constantly generating new space, thermodynamics dictates a strict accounting of energy. When any supercooled fluid freezes into a solid lattice, it undergoes an exothermic phase transition, releasing \textbf{Latent Heat of Fusion} into the surrounding environment.

Let the discrete quantum of latent thermal energy released by the genesis of a single $\mathcal{M}_A$ node be $E_f$. The continuous power density ($\mathcal{P}_{genesis}$) injected into the universe by the background expansion is:
\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{P}_{genesis} = \frac{\dot{N}}{V} E_f = 3H_0 \frac{N}{V} E_f
\end{equation}

Conversely, the physical expansion of the volumetric space causes a constant adiabatic cooling (Cosmological Redshift) of the existing background radiation ($u_{rad}$). From the standard continuity equation, the rate of cooling is exactly $-4H_0 u_{rad}$.

To define the thermodynamic steady-state of the cosmos, we equate the rate of continuous Latent Heat injection to the rate of adiabatic cooling:
\begin{equation}
    \dot{u}_{rad} = -4H_0 u_{rad} + \mathcal{P}_{genesis} = 0
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
    4H_0 u_{rad} = 3H_0 \frac{N}{V} E_f \implies u_{rad} = \frac{3}{4} \frac{N}{V} E_f
\end{equation}

By applying the Stefan-Boltzmann law ($u_{rad} = \frac{4\sigma}{c}T^4$), we strictly derive the equilibrium temperature of the universe:
\begin{equation}
    T_{CMB} = \left( \frac{3 N c}{16 \sigma V} E_f \right)^{1/4}
\end{equation}

\textbf{Conclusion:} The Cosmic Microwave Background ($2.725$ K) is \textit{not} a 13.8-billion-year-old fading relic of a primordial Big Bang explosion. It is the real-time, steady-state \textbf{Latent Heat of Crystallization}. The vacuum glows in the microwave spectrum today because new space is actively freezing into existence today. 

Furthermore, this thermodynamic proof completely abolishes the ``Heat Death of the Universe'' paradox. The universe will never freeze to absolute zero; it is structurally locked into a permanent $2.7$K thermal attractor state maintained by the latent heat of the spacetime engine.
