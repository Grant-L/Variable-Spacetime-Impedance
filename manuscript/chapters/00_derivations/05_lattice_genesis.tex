By the First Law of Thermodynamics, if the vacuum creates volume \textit{and} expels heat, it must fund both from its own mechanical expansion work ($P_{tot} dV$). \begin{equation}

\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
-P_{tot} dV = \rho_{vac} dV + \rho_{latent} dV \implies P_{tot} = -(\rho_{vac} + \rho_{latent})
\end{equation} 
This mathematically proves that the mechanical pressure of the vacuum must be strictly \textit{more negative} than $-\rho_{vac}$ to balance the ledger. Calculating the equation of state natively derives \textbf{Phantom Dark Energy}:
\begin{equation}
w_{vac} = \frac{P_{tot}}{\rho_{vac}} = \frac{-(\rho_{vac} + \rho_{latent})}{\rho_{vac}} = -1 - \frac{\rho_{latent}}{\rho_{vac}} < -1
\end{equation}
