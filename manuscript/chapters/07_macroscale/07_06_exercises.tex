\section{Exercises}
\label{sec:ch7_exercises}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Refractive Index Calculation:} Find the Lattice Stress $\sigma$ required to achieve an effective velocity of $2c$ relative to a stationary observer[cite: 615].
    
    \item \textbf{Tapering Geometry:} Design an impedance gradient profile that minimizes reflective loss (Cherenkov emission) at a bubble boundary traveling at $0.9c$[cite: 616].
    
    \item \textbf{Inertia Nullification Proof:} Show that a perfectly shielded metric bubble ($Z_{internal} = Z_{external}$) prevents the induction of Back-EMF during acceleration[cite: 579].
    
    \item \textbf{Short-Circuit Power:} Using hardware constants from Chapter 1, estimate the energy density (Joules/$\mu m^3$) yielded by a topological short in a ground-state vacuum[cite: 617].
\end{enumerate}
