\chapter{Universal Operators: Z, $\sigma$, $\Gamma$}
\label{ch:universal_operators}

The complete derivation chain spans 14 orders of magnitude
in length scale (from the lattice pitch $\ell_{node} \approx 3.86 \times
10^{-13}$~m to the Hubble radius $R_H \approx 1.33 \times 10^{26}$~m).
A persistent question is whether these scales are merely analogical---linked
by suggestive but structurally unrelated mathematics---or whether the
\textit{same operator} acts at every scale.

This book proves the latter.

\section{The Universal Saturation Operator}
\label{sec:universal_saturation}

The saturation factor
\begin{equation}
    \sigma(A, A_c) = \sqrt{1 - (A/A_c)^2}
    \label{eq:saturation_sigma}
\end{equation}
appears in four structurally distinct domains:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Dielectric saturation} (Axiom 4): lattice permittivity
          collapses under strong-field loading.
    \item \textbf{BCS critical field}: $B_c(T) = B_{c0}\sqrt{1-(T/T_c)^2}$.
    \item \textbf{Galactic rotation}: $\eta_{eff} = \eta_0\sqrt{1-(g_N/a_0)^2}$.
    \item \textbf{Relativistic mass--energy}: $E = mc^2/\sqrt{1-(v/c)^2}$.
\end{enumerate}

All four call \textit{the same engine function}
(\texttt{scale\_invariant.saturation\_factor(A, A\_yield)}) that serves
all four applications.

\section{The Universal Reflection Coefficient}
\label{sec:universal_gamma}

The transmission-line reflection coefficient
\begin{equation}
    \Gamma = \frac{Z_2 - Z_1}{Z_2 + Z_1}
    \label{eq:universal_gamma}
\end{equation}
appears at every impedance boundary in the framework:

\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\toprule
\textbf{Scale} & \textbf{Boundary} & \textbf{$\Gamma$ value} \\
\midrule
Sub-nuclear & Pauli exclusion ($Z_{knot} \to 0$) & $\Gamma \to -1$ \\
Laboratory  & PONDER-01 antenna port S$_{11}$ & $|\Gamma| < 1$ \\
Planetary   & Moho discontinuity (crust$\to$mantle) & $\Gamma \approx 0.17$ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

All seven applications call \textit{the same function}
(\texttt{scale\_invariant.reflection\_coefficient(Z1, Z2)}),
confirming that the particle physics of Pauli exclusion, the
geophysics of seismic reflection, and the astrophysics of
neutrino flavor conversion are structurally identical operations
on the $\mathcal{M}_A$ lattice impedance.
