\section*{Key Terms}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Borromean Confinement:} The topological linkage of three flux loops (quarks) such that removing one unlocks the others. The mechanical origin of the Strong Force.
    \item \textbf{Impedance Clamping:} The infinite resistance encountered by a signal (Right-Handed Neutrino) that violates the lattice grain.
    \item \textbf{Lattice Genesis:} The continuous crystallization of new nodes from the substrate, perceived as Cosmic Expansion.
    \item \textbf{Lattice Pitch ($l_P$):} The fundamental pixel size of the universe (Planck Length).
    \item \textbf{Metric Refraction:} The bending of flux trajectories due to node density gradients (Gravity) rather than geometric curvature.
    \item \textbf{Topological Helicity ($h$):} The quantized winding number of a defect, identified as Electric Charge.
\end{itemize}
