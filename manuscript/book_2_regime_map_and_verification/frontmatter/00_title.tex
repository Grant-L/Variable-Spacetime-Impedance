\noindent\textbf{Applied Vacuum Engineering: Volume II} \\
This volume verifies the structural identity of AVE operators across 40 orders of magnitude in length scale.

\begin{abstract}
    The preceding six volumes derive the complete physical framework from three bounding limits and four axioms. This volume poses the critical question: \textit{does the same mathematics work at every scale?}

    Nine domains are tested --- from galactic rotation curves through planetary magnetospheres, stellar interiors, gravitational wave propagation, superconductor phase transitions, seismic wave reflection, and protein backbone folding --- using the \textit{same three functions}: \texttt{impedance()}, \texttt{saturation\_factor()}, and \texttt{reflection\_coefficient()}.

    Every prediction uses zero adjustable parameters. Every computation calls the same code path. The result is not analogy --- it is \textbf{structural identity}.
\end{abstract}
